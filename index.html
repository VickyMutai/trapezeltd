    Our Story

    Starting with only one truck in 1982 as a sole proprietorship, the company has expanded to over 20 vehicles to keep pace with the rising need in the transport industry and more specifically utility industry.

    Our Vision

    To be a leader in service provision that exceeds client’s expectations in the Transport industry by continually striving to provide quality cargo, car hire, fleet management and transport services on time.

    Our Mission

    Our goal is to be of service to our principals with full dedication and providing them with fast, efficient, reliable and quality transport services.

    Core Values

  1. Maintaining service excellence
  2. Treating staff and customers with respect and honesty
  3. Offering customers a dedicated,personalized service.

    4. Services

    Our main aim is to create a deep relationship with our clients and partners. We specialize in:

    • Car hire services
    • Hire of equipment and machinery
    • Load consolidation services
    • Receiving and dispatching of goods
    • Local collections and deliveries to various warehouses for load consolidation
    • Insurance on goods to the value of the declared invoices
    • Dealing with and transporting hazardous goods
    • Dealing with heavy load
